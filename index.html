Services We Offer

Cleaning services are broadly categorized into residential (homes) and commercial (businesses/offices).

Regular House Cleaning

Regular house cleaning is a routine, maintenance service designed to keep a home tidy and hygienic, typically performed weekly or fortnightly. It focuses on high-traffic areas, including vacuuming, mopping, dusting, surface wiping, and bathroom/kitchen sanitization.

Commercial Cleaning

Commercial cleaning involves specialized, high-traffic maintenance for business environments, including offices, medical facilities, industrial sites, and retail spaces. Key examples include routine office janitorial work, deep cleaning, carpet and floor care, high-level window cleaning, and specialized sanitation for childcare or medical facilities

Office Cleaning

Professional office cleaning services ensure a hygienic, productive workplace through regular, scheduled cleaning of workspaces, kitchens, and restrooms. Services generally include vacuuming, mopping, dusting, trash removal, and disinfecting high-touch areas. Costs vary by location and sizes.
